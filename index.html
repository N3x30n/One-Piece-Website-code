Intro to One Piece

One piece is an anime and manga series from japan. The manga started in 1997, and the anime started in 1999. Author Eiichiro Oda started One piece because of his inspiration from a childhood TV show called ‘Vicky the Viking’. Currently One Piece has 1093 chapters and the anime has 1077 episodes and is still currently airing and being produced. One piece has its own type of power scaling when it comes to its characters. There are 3 forms of ‘combat’ in a sense. The first and main one are things called Devil Fruits. These are basically fruits that give the user special powers that they can manipulate in combat. For example the Mera Mera No Mi also known as the Flame fruit turns the users body into fire they can manipulate and use to their advantage such as shooting flaming bullets from their fingers or turning the soles of their feet into fire to propel themselves into the air. Another devil fruit that is used in the series is the Ito Ito No Mi also known as the String fruit. This allows the user to generate and manipulate wafer-like, razor-sharp strings that can attach to almost any surface.

Main Characters

Monkey D. Luffy:



Monkey D. Luffy or just Luffy is the Captain of the Straw Hat Pirates. He weilds the Gomu Gomu No Mi also known as the Gum Gum Fruit. This turns his body into basically rubber allowing him to stretch his body like rubber and makes him immune to most projectiles and blunt attacks. This allows him to create special moves that utilize this power to create stonger and more powerful attacks because of his rubber-like body.

Roronoa Zoro:


Roronoa Zoro is the vice captian of the Straw Hat Pirates. Zoro weilds 3 swords and uses the Santoryu Style or the Three-Sword style. This allows him to use special techniques that couldn't be accomplished with just two swords. He uses no Devil Fruit abilites and only uses swords.

Vinsmoke Sanji


Vinsmoke Sanji also known as 'Black Leg' Sanji is a member of the Straw Hat Pirates and their chef. He does not use a devil fruit and uses a fighting style called Black Leg. This fighting style is made of very powerful kicks that have the capacity to destroy mountains with a single kick. This makes him a very capable fighter and one of the main fighters for the Straw Hats
